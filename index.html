How It All Began

Our Story

✦

We are Mark and Kimberly, and our story first began in November 2006 through a mutual friend at a coffee shop in Timog, QC. Mark admittedly thought Kim was cute — especially her beautiful hair. What started as a simple friendship quickly turned into hours of phone calls, endless conversations, and a connection neither of us expected.

What began as a simple "Okay, let's try being together" slowly became a lifetime promise of love, partnership, and forever.

Mark Anthony & Kimberly
Twenty Years of Choosing Each Other

Our Journey

✦
2006
☕

Where It All Started

From that moment on, our lives became filled with shared adventures — long drives with no particular destination, spontaneous out-of-town trips, food trips, movie dates, massage dates, matching outfits, doing laundry together, cooking experiments, and quiet evenings filled with laughter and comfort in each other's company.

💍

The Proposal

Nine years later, on a cold and drizzly afternoon at Victoria Peak in Hong Kong, Mark got down on one knee and asked Kim to marry him. Of course, Kim said yes. Soon after, we were married in a civil ceremony, beginning another beautiful chapter of our lives together.

2015
The Distance
✈️

Through Every Season

Like any relationship, our journey was not always easy. We spent years navigating a long-distance relationship before finally being reunited and building our life together as husband and wife. Through every season — both joyful and difficult — we continued choosing one another, again and again.

🌿

Faith, Love & Marquin

One of the happiest moments of our lives was finding out we were expecting our first baby. Though we later experienced the heartbreak of miscarriage, our faith, love, and unwavering support for one another carried us through. Together, we continued to grow, pursue our dreams and careers, and stand side by side through every challenge life placed before us.

Holding tightly to Isaiah 60:22 — "When the time is right, I, the Lord, will make it happen" — in God's perfect timing, we were blessed with our son, Marquin, now two years old and one of our greatest blessings.

Marquin
⛪

November 8, 2026

Today, as we prepare to be married in church surrounded by the people we love most, we also celebrate another beautiful milestone — 20 years of partnership, growing together, and finding home in one another through every chapter of life.

As we stand on the edge of our greatest adventure yet, we look forward to continuing to write the rest of our story — hand in hand, side by side, and together forever.

Habambuhay, ikaw at ako — sa hirap at ginhawa,
kasama ang Diyos sa gitna ng ating pagmamahalan
at sa ating munting tahanan.

Mark Anthony & Kimberly

Questions & Answers

Things to Know

✦
Where is the ceremony held?
The ceremony will be held at the Chapel of the Transfiguration, Calaruega, 4231 Caleruega Road, Nasugbu, Batangas, Philippines — beginning at 6:00 PM.
Where is the reception?
The reception will be at Palacio Crystal Del Aquila, Happy Rd, Kaybagal South, Tagaytay City, 4120 Cavite. Guest arrival begins at 5:00 PM, ceremony at 6:00 PM, cocktail hour at 7:30 PM, dinner at 8:30 PM, and after party at 10:30 PM.
What is the dress code?
We invite you to dress in Modern Filipiniana style — Barong Tagalog, Polo or Long Sleeves with Slacks, Filipiniana, or a Puff-Sleeved Gown or Dress in our palette: Champagne, Sand, Taupe, Sage Green, Olive, Terracotta, Walnut, or Brass. Please avoid White, Ivory, Dark Colors, and Denim.
When is the RSVP deadline?
Please kindly RSVP by October 8, 2026, so we can prepare every detail of our celebration for all our beloved guests.
Can guests take photos?
Yes! Guests are welcome to take photos anytime throughout the celebration. We only ask for mindful and respectful presence during the ceremony.
Can I bring a plus-one?
If your invitation includes a plus-one, it will be reflected on your RSVP. Thank you for understanding our limited seating.
Should I fill out the RSVP if I can't attend?
We'll truly miss celebrating with you! If you can't make it, you may simply let us know directly.
